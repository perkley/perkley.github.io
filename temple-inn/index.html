How Can We Help You?

Whether you are serving in the temple or participating in an event, like a sealing or youth trip, we are here to help you.

We specialize in hotel accomodations near the temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Our mission is to provide unique services for temple patrons, families, and visiting friends.

Some of our Services

  • Changing rooms to get into church clothes
  • Short-term babysitting
  • Long-term stay options
  • Family history resources
  • Reception hall
  • Catering

We strive to provide an atmosphere where you can feel the spirit and provide for your individual, family or friends needs in a reverent and organized way.
