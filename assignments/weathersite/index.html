Wild weather could include hail, snow and tornados in SE Idaho today and tomorrow

By SCOTT KRAUS For the Citizen

Severe thunderstorms, hail, strong winds, heavy rain and unseasonably cold temperatures are possible in East Idaho starting Thursday morning, according to the National Weather Service.
